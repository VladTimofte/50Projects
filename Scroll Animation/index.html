Steps in living a God's will life

Admit that you are a sinner

See the need to be saved

Understand that only Jesus can save you

Know the truth that there is nothing you can do to be saved by your own facts

Accept the gift that Jesus gives you by his death

Accept him in your life

If happens to fall, do not be discouraged

Continue your way, as Jesus loves you

You are the most precious thing on earth for him

He can't wait to see you

Tell others about how He changed your life

Seek His will daily

Enjoy His Grace

Live in the truth

Love everyone

Forgive as He forgave you

Grow spiritually

Be a light

Wait for Him
